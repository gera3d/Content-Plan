## SEO Best Practices Applied

### Title Tags
- ✅ Under 60 characters (displays fully in search)
- ✅ Includes target keyword near the beginning
- ✅ Includes year [2025] for freshness
- ✅ Compelling and clear value proposition
- ✅ Unique for each page

### Meta Descriptions
- ✅ 150-160 characters (optimal length)
- ✅ Includes target keywords naturally
- ✅ Clear value proposition
- ✅ Call-to-action implied
- ✅ Accurate representation of content
- ✅ Unique for each page

### Open Graph Tags
- ✅ Optimized for social sharing
- ✅ 1200x630px images (Facebook/LinkedIn standard)
- ✅ Compelling descriptions for social
- ✅ Article type with publish dates
- ✅ Author attribution

### Twitter Cards
- ✅ Large image format (summary_large_image)
- ✅ Optimized descriptions (shorter than meta)
- ✅ High-quality preview images
- ✅ Creator attribution
