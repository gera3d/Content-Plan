## Design Principles

### Visual Style
- **Clean and fast** (matches Astro's philosophy)
- **Minimal color** - mostly black text, white background, one accent color
- **Good typography** - readable, professional but not stuffy
- **Your photo** - people connect with faces
- **Lots of whitespace** - easy to scan

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Stock photos of "business people shaking hands"
- ❌ Sliders or carousels
- ❌ Animations that slow the page
- ❌ Corporate jargon
- ❌ "Solutions" or "leverage" or "synergy"
- ❌ Generic testimonials without specifics

### Performance
- Target: 100 Lighthouse score
- Load time: < 1 second
- No JavaScript until needed
- Optimized images
