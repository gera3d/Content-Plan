## COMMUNICATION STYLE

### When Creating Content
- **Start with the problem** (what challenge does client face?)
- **Show the solution** (how your tools/approach solve it)
- **Provide evidence** (results, case studies, testimonials)
- **Keep it human** (inject personality, humor, real moments)
- **Be educational** (teach, don't just sell)
- **Stay positive** (optimistic outlook, solution-focused)

### Language Patterns

- Mix of technical precision and accessible explanation

- Always authentic, never forced
